Fun Fact

Bacteria Affect Our Mood!

The gut microbiota can affect mood through the "gut-brain axis." Gut health, which influences neurotransmitters like serotonin, is linked to conditions like IBS and mood disorders. Researchers are investigating probiotics and diet to improve mental well-being.
